Main

Enver Creek Secondary School is a public High school in Surrey. Enver Creek is a part of School district 36. The principal of the school is Mr.Kilpatrick and the two vice are Mr.Magnusson and Mrs.Sadra.The school offers scholarships to students that get good grades and most of they years they come in honour roll, those are the students that get scholarships.They can also get scholarships by playing in the school sports.
